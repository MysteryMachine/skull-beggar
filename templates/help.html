You are free to bet on 4 different things. Firstly, you can select the boss you think will end the run. If the caster dies to that boss, you will win coins. Envy, Greed, etc. count as bosses. Search for boss to see these.

You can bet on the floor the caster will die on. If the caster dies to a non-boss, then they should award money to the people who guessed what floor they would die on. Search for floor to see these.

You can also bet on Sucide and Win. On the chance that the player kills themself, or they win, they will award money to people who guessed those two options. Search for Win or Suicide.

If you died to a specific boss, select that boss. If you died to a non-boss enemy, select the floor in which you died. If you killed yourself, select Suicide. If you won, select Win.
