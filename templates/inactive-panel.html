Summary

In Skull Beggar, the streamer opens betting before beginning a Binding of Isaac game. Users are allowed to place a bet on the enemy they believe will be responsible for the streamer's death. The user is also allowed to bet on the streamer winning the game. The streamer then must close betting, and play the game until they win or lose. If some users guessed correctly, the pot is split evenly between them. If not, bets are returned to the users.
